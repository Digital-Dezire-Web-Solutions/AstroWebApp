    Astrology reveals the will of the God

    At Astro Nivesh, (Owned By: Shriram Lalasaheb Magar) we view astrology as a time-honored predictive science that has long provided profound insights into various aspects of life. Its blend of ancient wisdom and precise methods continues to inspire and guide believers, even as modern perspectives evolve

    At its core, astrology is the study of cosmic influences—examining how stars and planets shape our lives. While modern astronomy identifies eight planets, traditional astrology recognizes nine key celestial bodies known as the Navagrahas. Intriguingly, Earth is not included in this celestial lineup.

    The Navagrahas are: •⁠ ⁠Sun (Surya) •⁠ ⁠Moon (Chandra) •⁠ ⁠Mars (Mangala) •⁠ ⁠Mercury (Budha) •⁠ ⁠Jupiter (Brihaspati) •⁠ ⁠Venus (Shukra) •⁠ ⁠Saturn (Shani) •⁠ ⁠Rahu (North Lunar Node) •⁠ ⁠Ketu (South Lunar Node)

    Each of these planets plays a distinct role in your life. Some are considered benevolent, bestowing positive energies and opportunities, while others, like Rahu and Ketu, can sometimes signal challenges. However, the influence of any planet is never absolute—it depends greatly on its placement within the twelve houses of your Kundli.

    For instance, the first house, or ascendant, represents your personality and physical traits, setting the tone for your entire life. Similarly, the fifth house, known as Putra Bhava, governs creativity, joy, romance, and playfulness. A favorable planet like Jupiter in the fifth house can enhance your love life and creative pursuits, whereas an adverse influence from Rahu might prompt the need for deeper insight and guidance.

    Beyond these basics, astrology encompasses a rich array of elements—from Moon signs and numerology to tarot and more. At Astro Nivesh, we integrate these diverse aspects to offer you comprehensive, personalized insights that empower your financial growth and personal well-being.
