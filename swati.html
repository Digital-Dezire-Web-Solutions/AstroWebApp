    Introduction

    Swati Nakshatra spans from 6°40′ to 20°00′ in Libra. It is symbolized by a young plant blowing in the wind, reflecting flexibility, independence, and movement. Ruled by Rahu and presided over by Vayu, the god of wind, this nakshatra is associated with freedom, adaptability, and learning through exploration.

    Personality Traits
    • Independent, adaptable, and freedom-loving
    • Soft-spoken, polite, and peace-seeking
    • Strong desire to learn and improve
    • Drawn to travel, discovery, and unique experiences
    • Can oscillate between confidence and self-doubt
    Strengths
    • Charming, diplomatic, and socially graceful
    • Flexible and able to navigate challenges with ease
    • Good at communication and negotiation
    • Resilient and open-minded
    Challenges
    • Restless and indecisive at times
    • Can be too accommodating or easily influenced
    • Struggles with commitment or consistency
    • May hide true intentions behind charm
